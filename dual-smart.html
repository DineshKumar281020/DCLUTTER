DualSmart

Automated Medication and Supply Management System

Would you like to Reduce Your Costs while facilitating patient and medication safety?

DualSMART, brings together MedSMART and SupplySMART on a single platform and allows medication and supplies to be managed from a single station and a single touch screen.

It has all the features of MedSMART and SupplySMART and while supplies are managed with Take-Return technology, medications are managed order-based.

System Features

  • Medications and supplies available at stations can only be accessed by authorized personnel through BIO ID authentication.
  • Lighted Path illuminated guidance helps the user to find materials easily.
  • For medication requiring high security, controlled access to unit dose is allowed while Touch-to-Take technology enables easy and fast access to necessary supplies.
  • Once a nurse takes the medication/supply and removes it out of the system, that item is dropped from the inventory and the patient is exclusively invoiced for the medication/supply.
  • System automatically sends information to the pharmacy for the medication and main warehouse for the supply at or below minimum level.
  • System calculates quantitative inventory cycle speed and optimizes inventory levels.
  • The DualSMART configuration solution can be extended by adding auxiliary Med, Supply, and DualTowers.
  • The variety of drawers in size and type gives DualSMART the ability to manage different medications at different security levels.

Solutions that Create Value for all Healthcare Professionals

DualSMART solutions offer an increase in the quality of healthcare, patient and employee satisfaction. They enable your institution to track, report, and analyze all medical transactions and allow long-term planning.

How do we relieve your worries concerning patient safety?
  • By enabling controlled access to all medications and supplies at wards
  • By facilitating the clinical role of the pharmacists
  • By warning users expiry dates before taking an item
  • By monitoring in-cabin temperature and humidity values
  • By reducing administration time of initial dose
  • By enabling access to the right medication ordered for the patient
How do we facilitate employee satisfaction?
  • By defining kits to enable that several medications/supplies can be taken from the system at a time
  • By immediately adapting to the CV orders that are changed or cancelled during the day
  • By relieving nurses from their duties concerning inventory management
  • By submitting invoice and inventory operations simultaneously
How do we reduce your costs?
  • By including medication and supply inventories of the wards with secure access
  • By reducing waste medications arising from expiry dates
  • By automating management of returns
  • By enabling consignment, re-use and bill-only products within automatic supply management systems

With DualSMART All Data is at Your Fingertips...

SMARTReport

It is an advanced reporting portal preserving the operations performed at DualSMART products and presenting these to users.

It is a business intelligence platform transforming all data generated by DualSMART products into integrated and meaningful data and presenting these to users.
