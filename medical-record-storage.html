KANBAN MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

The DCLUTTER® Kanban Management System is a complete modular and scalable high density storage solution (cabinet or shelf + modular assembly set according to ISO3394 / HTM71), which incorporates the Kanban Box and Kanban Manager software for an online, organized and objective management of fungible material across hospital warehouses.

Operating Cycle

Service Warehouse

1. STOCK: Double Compartment Agreed Stock

2. PICKING: Picking the Product (anterior compartment)

3. ORDER: Empty Drawer Equals Order Made

4. WHILE: Picking the Product (posterior compartment)

8. REPLENISHMENT (2nd Step): Product Restocking

Central Warehouse

5. ORDER PREPARATION: Order Scanning

6. TRANSPORT: Transport to the Service Warehouse

7. REPLENISHMENT (1st Step): Transfers the Product in Use

Urgent Orders

Urgent Orders (replenishment of both compartments): When the nursing staff become aware of a possible stock shortage, they remove the red tag (from the second drawer) located in the drawer currently in use, and insert it into the Kanban Box MMSlog® which will detect that an urgent order has been made and the product will be ordered automatically for the warehouse/unit, so that the replenishment will arrive within 24 hours.
