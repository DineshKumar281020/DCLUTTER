About Smart Dispensing Pharmacy
Overview
The hospital pharmacy main function is to be responsible for monitoring the supply of all medicines used across the hospital. The pharmacy is also responsible for purchasing, storing, distributing and quality testing themedications.
The pharmacy handles between 3,000 to 15,000 SKUs (Stock keeping units) and efficiently managing the same is a big challenge. Pharmacy gives face lift to the hospital since the patients get direct interaction with the department.
The pharmacy, well presented & maintained will sure to gain greater confidence with the patients. In fact Pharmacy is one of the major revenue generating department of the hospital.
